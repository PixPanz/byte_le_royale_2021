Add On Objects¶

Add-On object occupies the addons attribute of your truck. You can only have one Add-On object at a time.

The Police Scanner Object¶

The police scanner object helps you evade the cops. It will reduce the damage and time taken from the police event. Referenced as:

ObjectType.policeScanner

The Rabbits Foot Object¶

The Rabbit’s foot will bring you good luck in the event a disaster occuring. It will minorly reduce incoming damage from all event types. This bonus will stack additively with your other damage reduction bonuses. Referenced as:
